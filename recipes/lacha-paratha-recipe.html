Lacha Paratha Recipe

Lacha Paratha is a popular North Indian layered flatbread known for its flaky texture and delicious taste. It's a perfect accompaniment to a variety of curries and dishes. Here's a simple recipe to make Lacha Paratha:

Ingredients:

  • Paratha Dough:
    1. 2 cups whole wheat flour (atta)
    2. Water, as needed
    3. 1/4 teaspoon salt
    4. 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Layering:
    1. 2 tablespoons ghee or butter
    2. Additional flour for dusting
