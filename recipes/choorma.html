Choorma Recipe

Choorma is a traditional North Indian sweet dish made from coarsely ground wheat flour and is often served during festive occasions and celebrations.
It's typically made using ghee (clarified butter) and jaggery, along with optional additions like nuts and cardamom for flavor. Here's a basic recipe for making choorma:

Ingredients:
  • Flour
    1. 1 cup whole wheat flour (gehun ka atta)
    2. Water, as needed
    3. Ghee, for deep frying
  • Jaggery syrup
    1. 1 cup jaggery, grated or broken into small pieces
    2. 1/2 cup water
    3. A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
    4. Cardamom pods, crushed, or cardamom powder, to taste
  • Optional additions:
    1. Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios, cashews)
    2. Raisins
  • Chocolate milk
  • Add this milk into it and stir
