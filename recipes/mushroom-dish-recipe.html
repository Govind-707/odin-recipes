Mushroom Dish Recipe

This flavorful mushroom dish is a delicious option for a savory meal. Made with fresh mushrooms and aromatic spices, it's perfect for any occasion. Here's how to prepare it:

Ingredients:

  • Mushrooms
    1. 250g fresh mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
    2. 2 tablespoons olive oil
    3. 1 onion, finely chopped
    4. 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Spice Mix
    1. 1 teaspoon cumin powder
    2. 1 teaspoon paprika
    3. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    4. Salt to taste
  • Fresh Herbs
    1. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    2. 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • Optional Additions:
    1. Grated parmesan cheese
    2. Red chili flakes
